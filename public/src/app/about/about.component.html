What is Jackpot?

Wealthsimple's Jackpot products are an innovative way to help you save, linking the fun and excitement of winning a lottery to savings.

It's a win-win for YOU - you set some money aside into our special savings account, and earn chances to win prizes. While there are many prize winners, those who don't win a prize are winners too because they walk away with the savings they set aside.

Jackpot is an exciting way to potentially win the lottery, without any risk!
